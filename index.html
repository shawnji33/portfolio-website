I'm a product designer and my work spans fintech, AI tools, and complex B2B products. I went deep on product design after studying interaction design and after several years at startups I now work independently with founders and companies.


Since then I've been lucky to work with ambitious teams — from seed-stage startups to companies of thousands — shipping products that made real things feel effortless.

I use AI tools — Cursor, Codex, Claude Code — as everyday parts of my workflow. I care about systems thinking: getting the architecture right, not just the pixels. I work closely with engineers and believe good design and good engineering are the same discipline.

I consider myself incredibly lucky to have shipped products people actually use. I'd love to work on your hardest design problems.
